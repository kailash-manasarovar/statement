# 1993 

## Meeting Matthew Copeland in dreams

### Matthew

- Matthew and I fell in love in probably January of 1993.
- We were out raving.
- He had the same birthday as my brother.
- THE COINCIDENCE WAS HUGE!!!
- I have to wonder now if it's really his birthday.
- He told me his dad was a porn-king.
- I thought he was being an idiot.
- I fell out of love with him as soon as the brute showed up, which was not delayed.
- Nevertheless, always in good spirit, we remained friends over the years, at least that's what I thought.
- It's only now I wonder if Matthew assumed that because we'd had sex, he owned me, like everyone seems to do with women - having sex with them not even necessary - and especially with the *special* ones.

### Meeting in dreams

- I'd been devouring Castaneda around that time.
- If you're unfamiliar, it's packed full of lucid dreaming techniques and parallel universes.
- I said to Matthew, when we were in love, *why don't we meet in dreams?*.
- He was interested.
- I explained.
- We pick a time and place, when we're asleep, and we agree to meet there.
- We picked the top of my road, at the lamppost there, at 3am.
- That night, I wake up to the sound of the front door knocking.
- I run downstairs.
- I open the front door.
- Matthew is standing there.
- It's Matthew, but he's a bunch of electrical wires, disconnecting, misfiring, broken, disordered, totally chaotic.

![Matthew at the front door](../../content/images/matthew-asking-for-help.png)

- He says my name: *Katie*.
- I'm horrified, and wake up.
- I tell everyone about my dream the next day, including Matthew.
- He lies and says, yes he was there, but he would not have been conscious of what I saw, if it was him, or he would have been a different person completely.
