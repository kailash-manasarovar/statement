# May 2022

## Asked to be a demo at the conservatory

- Paqui Fornet contacts me out of the blue and asks if I can do her a favor.
- She wants me to come and help out as they have a man from the Generalitat coming in doing a demonstration to everyone of film technology; how to film concerts etc.
- She tells me they need someone to be a demo piano player.
- I don't think anything unusual is going on and agree.
- I go in and there are about 25 male teachers and 5 female teachers in the concert room at the conservatory.
- They ask me to play while the video cameraman gives a lecture and demonstration on how to film musical performances properly.
- I'm a bit nervous but I'm happy to help.
- Given what happens over the next couple of years, I have to wonder if this was an early advertisement of a new switcheroo victim coming up.
